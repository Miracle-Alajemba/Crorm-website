Welcome,Kids

Hey there! We’re so glad you’re here! This is a special place to learn about God’s amazing love for YOU! God Made You Amazing! Did you know you’re one of a kind? God made you wonderfully unique, and He wants you to know Him from a young age. He has big dreams for you, and He’s with you every step of the way! Jesus, Your Forever Friend Jesus is the best friend you could ask for. He teaches us to love, be kind, and make a difference. By learning about Jesus now, you’re starting a life long journey with Him, just like God says to "catch them young!" Bible Stories & Fun Activities Dive into stories about heroes like David, Esther, and Jesus! Plus, enjoy coloring, puzzles, and quizzes to help you learn about God’s love while having fun. Prayer: Talking to God Anytime Talking to God is easy just say what’s on your mind! Whether you’re happy, sad, or thankful, God loves hearing from you anytime. Grow with Us! Come back often for more stories, games, and ways to shine God’s light. Remember, God loves you so much and has an incredible plan for you!

Youth

Hey, Students! This space is for you discover God’s plan and find encouragement as you navigate life and school! You’re Here for a Reason God made you unique and has a purpose for you. Jesus: Your Guide Jesus isn’t just a figure He’s with you, guiding you through ups and downs and showing you how to live with courage and love. The Bible: Real Wisdom The Bible is full of advice for real life on friendships, challenges, and choices. It’s got what you need to thrive. Prayer: Your Connection Prayer is just talking to God. Share anything your dreams, worries, or thanks. He’s always there to listen. Let’s Grow Together! You’re chosen, you’re loved, and you’re here to make a difference. Let’s go after it!
